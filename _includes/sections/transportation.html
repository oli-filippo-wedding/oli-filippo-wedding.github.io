Flights

From Italy: there are direct flights from Venice Treviso (WizzAir, Wednesdays and Sundays), Milan Malpensa (WizzAir, Mondays and Fridays) and Roma Ciampino (WizzAir, Mondays and Fridays).

From Switzerland: there are direct flights from Zurich (Edelweiss SwissAir, Air Pristina/Germania Flug, every day) and Basel (WizzAir, every day).

The following airlines cover flights to Skopje from other destinations: WizzAir, Austrian Airlines, Air Serbia, Adria Airways and Turkish Airlines.


Airport to Hotel

The airport is 20 km away from the center of Skopje. To reach Skopje, we would recommend getting a taxi from the airport (1220 MKD = ~ 20 EUR). The hotel also offers the option to organize a van for pick up if you are more people (6-7) arriving at the same time (~ 50 EUR).
